History of Modern Design

Early Modernism

The roots of modern design can be traced back to:

  • • The Industrial Revolution (1760-1840)
  • • Bauhaus Movement (1919-1933)
  • • International Style (1920s-1930s)

Key Milestones

  • 1920s Art Deco and Modernist Architecture
  • 1950s Mid-Century Modern Movement
  • 1980s Postmodern Design Era

Evolution

Modern design has continuously evolved, adapting to technological advancements and cultural changes. From the functional simplicity of early modernism to today's digital-first approach, the journey reflects our changing relationship with technology and aesthetics.
